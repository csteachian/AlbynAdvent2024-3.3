Day 5

Joke of the day:

Why did the wreath fail its driving test? Because it kept going in circles!

Christmas is an important time of year that many people around the

world celebrate. Families come together and continue old traditions and

make new ones, just like wreath making! Wreaths were big in Ancient

Egypt and Ancient Greece, as they were given to some Olympic athletes.

You can also make wreaths out of lots of things found in nature and in

your house. You can even spice things up by adding candles and ribbons!
