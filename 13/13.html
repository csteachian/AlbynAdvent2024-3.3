13th December

At Christmas, Superman's headquarters, the Fortress of Solitude, is transformed into a winter wonderland. The icy, crystalline walls of the fortress gleam under the soft glow of holiday lights, while snow gently falls outside. Inside, Superman decorates with care, placing a towering tree adorned with ornaments from around the world and hanging stockings for his closest allies. Despite his heroic duties, the holiday spirit fills the air, as even the Man of Steel takes a rare moment to enjoy the warmth of peace and goodwill.
